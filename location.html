Location Brief

Townfit is located in Oxford Street, central London. You can get there via the underground using the central and victoria line. Alternatively you can drive there as the gym has its own private car park. Fees for parking are charged at £1 per hour. We look forward to seeing you soon.

About Our location

Townfit Gym is situated in the heart of London, making it very accessible and convenient to get to. It is located next to Oxford Circus tube station and is surrounded by London's favourite restaurants,shops and bars. If you want to join Townfit please don't hesitate in filling out our registration form. It only takes a few minutes and you will be then on your way to achieving your personal fitness goals.

Parking

Townfit gym has its own car park available to its members. Parking is charged at £1 per hour and discounts are available if you have an annual membership with us.

For more enquiries about our parking spaces and accessing the gym please contact us via telephone or by email.
